OUR FOUNDER

OUR STORY

Samsung is a global leader in electronics and technology, founded in 1938 in South Korea. With a strong commitment to innovation and quality, Samsung has become one of the most recognized and trusted brands in the world. From smartphones to televisions, Samsung has continuously pushed the boundaries of what's possible in technology. With a focus on user experience and design, Samsung products seamlessly integrate into consumers' lives. At Samsung, we are dedicated to sustainability and social responsibility. We strive to minimize our environmental impact and promote responsible business practices throughout our global operations. Our mission is to inspire the world with our innovative products and services, while making a positive impact on society. We are proud to be a part of the Samsung family, and we look forward to shaping the future of technology for years to come.

FUTURE TARGET

Samsung's future goals are focused on continuing to innovate and bring cutting-edge technology to consumers worldwide. One of our main goals is to become a leader in the field of artificial intelligence (AI), with a focus on developing new products and services that utilize AI to enhance user experience and streamline daily tasks. In addition, we are committed to developing sustainable and eco-friendly products, with the goal of reducing our environmental impact and promoting responsible consumption. Another important aspect of our future goals is to foster a culture of diversity and inclusion within our company, with a focus on promoting gender equality and increasing the representation of underrepresented groups in technology. Overall, our mission is to continue to push the boundaries of what's possible in technology while remaining committed to sustainability.
